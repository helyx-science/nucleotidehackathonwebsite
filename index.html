About

NucleoTIDE is a 3 day virtual biotech hackathon hosted by The Helyx Initiative and is absolutely free to attend. Highschoolers and college students will compete to design a technological solution towards a certain theme.

There will be a variety of onsite mentors, workshops and other events for everyone to attend, as well as thousands in prizes to win!

As part of Major League Hacking, we will be following MLH's Code of Conduct.
